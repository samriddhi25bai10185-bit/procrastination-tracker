# Project Statement: Procrastination Tracker

## Problem Statement
Many students struggle with procrastination and inconsistent study habits. They often plan their study schedule but fail to follow it effectively. Without proper tracking, it becomes difficult to understand productivity patterns and identify areas for improvement.

## Proposed Solution
The Procrastination Tracker is a command-line Python application that helps users monitor their daily study habits by comparing planned study hours with actual study hours. It calculates a productivity score and provides feedback to encourage better time management.

## Objectives
- Track daily study performance
- Measure productivity using a simple scoring system
- Provide feedback to reduce procrastination
- Help users analyze their study patterns over time

## Target Users
- Students preparing for exams
- Beginners learning Python through practical projects
- Individuals aiming to improve productivity and discipline

## Key Functionality
- Input planned and actual study hours
- Store data using JSON
- Calculate productivity score
- Show analysis (average, best day, worst day)
- Provide warnings for low productivity

## Expected Outcome
Users will become more aware of their study habits, identify procrastination patterns, and gradually improve their productivity through consistent tracking and feedback.
